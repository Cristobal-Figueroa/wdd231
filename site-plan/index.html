Site Plan - Brew & Beans

Site Name

Brew & Beans

This name was selected because it perfectly represents the essence of an artisanal coffee shop that focuses on both the brewing process ("Brew") and the quality of coffee beans ("Beans"). It's a short, memorable name that clearly communicates the purpose of the business.

Potential domain: brewandbeans.com

Site Purpose

The Brew & Beans website will serve as a digital platform to:

  • Display the complete menu of beverages and food items, including prices and detailed descriptions.
  • Provide information about location, opening hours, and contact methods.
  • Share the history and philosophy behind the coffee shop.
  • Promote special events such as live music nights, barista workshops, and coffee tastings.
  • Offer a contact form for reservations, inquiries, and feedback.
  • Showcase a photo gallery of products and the shop's atmosphere.

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

A tourist visiting the city for the first time is looking for a cozy place to enjoy specialty coffee. They wonder: "Where can I find specialty coffee near my hotel and what are their opening hours?"

Scenario 2:

A local resident wants to organize a business meeting in a quiet environment. They wonder: "Does Brew & Beans offer free WiFi and have suitable tables for working? Can I make a reservation for a small group?"

Scenario 3:

A coffee enthusiast interested in learning more about brewing methods. They wonder: "What types of specialty coffee do they offer and do they host workshops or events related to coffee?"
